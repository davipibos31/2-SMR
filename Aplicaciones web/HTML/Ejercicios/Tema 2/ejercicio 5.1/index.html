Building MSF

Since 1980, MSF has opened offices in 28 countries and employs more than 30,000 people all over the world. From Foundation, MSF has treated more than 100 million of patients.

MSF has also maintained its institutional and financial independence, and the organization has remained review both from herself and from the help system in general when appropriate, all in the name of trying to help lead more effective and timely help to those who need it most.
