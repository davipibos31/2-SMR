Geography

Location

Located on the eastern coast of the Iberian Peninsula and the western part of the Mediterranean Sea, facing the Gulf of Valencia, Valencia sits on the highly fertile alluvial silts accumulated in the alluvial plain formed in the lower course of the river Turia.

weather

Valencia has a subtropical Mediterranean climate with mild winters and hot, dry summers. Snowfalls are extremely rare; The last time snow accumulated on the ground was on January 11, 1960.
