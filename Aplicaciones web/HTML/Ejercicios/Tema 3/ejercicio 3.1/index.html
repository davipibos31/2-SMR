Alicante

Among the most notable features of the city are the Santa Barbara castle , which is very above the city, and the Port of Alicante . The latter was the subject of bitter controversy in 2006-2007 when residents successfully fought to keep it from becoming an industrial estate.

The Castillo de Santa Barbara is located in Mount Benacantilla , overlooking the city. La Torre (La Torreta) in the part upper, is the oldest part of the castle, while part of the lower area and the walls are built later in the 18th century.
